Watergate Salad

Watergate Salad, a tasty holiday favorite!

Watergate Salad is a sweet, creamy dessert made from a mix of pistachio pudding, crushed pineapple, whipped topping, mini marshmallows, and chopped nuts. Known for its pale green color and light texture, it’s a popular dish at family gatherings and potlucks. Though its name hints at the Watergate scandal, there’s no confirmed connection, and it likely originated as a Kraft recipe from the 1970s.

Ingredients:

  • pistachio pudding
  • crushed pineapple
  • whipped topping
  • mini marshmallows
  • chopped nuts
  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. Mix Pineapple and Pudding
  3. Add Marshmallows and Nuts
  4. Fold in Whipped Topping
  5. Chill the Salad
  6. Serve
